Found & Ordered

A website built to be found — by Google and by AI answer engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity — not just a template with your logo on it. Add direct POS ordering and orders skip the delivery-app cut entirely: 84% of customers already say they'd rather order straight from a restaurant's own site than through a third-party app.

Pricing (starting at, single-location businesses):

  • Website only: $395 upfront, or $50/month for 9 months ($450 total)
  • Website + POS ordering integration: $450 upfront, or $50/month for 11 months ($550 total)

This covers up to 5 pages, standard template customization, and one round of revisions. Anything beyond that — additional pages, custom integrations beyond POS, extra rounds of revisions — is quoted separately, and we'll always tell you before doing any of that work.

Pricing above is for a single-location business. Multi-location groups or larger operations fall under Enterprise & Multi-Site Systems — let's talk about your setup.

Once paid off, the site is fully yours — no ongoing charges unless you want them. Most clients continue on an optional $50/month Care plan: ongoing AEO/SEO monitoring as AI search evolves, security patches, backups, uptime monitoring, and small content edits. It's the same $50/month you were already paying, just repurposed — and it's opt-in, never an automatic rollover.

Available add-ons: smart upsell/cross-sell prompts at checkout, loyalty & rewards program, QR-code/table ordering, catering & event booking, Google Business Profile setup, local citation building, monthly content refresh.

Best for: single-location restaurants, local and service businesses. Length: 2–4 weeks.

Never Miss a Call

Voice AI that answers when your team can't, takes the order or booking, and pushes it straight into your POS or CRM — so a busy lunch rush or an after-hours call never turns into a lost sale.

Available add-ons: AI chat/concierge widget for your site, email & SMS marketing automation, automated review requests and AI-drafted responses.

Best for: any business losing revenue to missed calls. Length: 2–4 weeks. Contact us for a quote based on call volume.

Paperwork Autopilot

An intake pipeline that reads your PDFs, contracts, and forms, pulls out the data automatically, and routes anything ambiguous to a person instead of guessing. Built on the same pattern that turned weeks of lease-abstraction review into same-day turnaround for one of our clients.

Available add-ons: inventory sync automation, automated ops/reporting dashboards, ongoing monthly processing retainer for continuous document flow.

Best for: real estate, professional services, back-office-heavy teams. Length: 2–4 weeks. Contact us for a quote based on document volume and complexity.

Enterprise & Multi-Site Systems

For corporate groups running multiple brands or subsidiaries: we unify them behind one CMS and one navigation experience — no more bouncing between separate open tabs to manage each site — and connect shared systems like career portals and payment platforms so they actually work as one.

Best for: corporate groups, multi-brand and multi-subsidiary businesses. Length: custom scope, typically 12+ weeks. Custom-quoted — let's talk about what you're running today.

Fractional CTO & Staff Coaching

For founders, engineering leaders, and organizations that need executive technical direction without hiring a full-time executive. Beyond high-level architecture and technical roadmaps, a core pillar of our fractional CTO retainer is direct staff coaching and developer up-skilling.

If your in-house developers or internal team are taking on AI integrations (MCP, LLM agent pipelines), modern full-stack workflows (React/TypeScript/Node), or system redesigns, we partner directly with them to elevate their skills, review pull requests, establish production governance, and unblock complex technical challenges.

What’s included:

  • Staff coaching & mentorship: 1:1 weekly engineering check-ins, architecture pair programming, and code review feedback.
  • Technical strategy & roadmap: Evaluating tech stacks, vendor tools, API architectures, and infrastructure scaling.
  • AI & modern web upskilling: Guiding your team on Model Context Protocol (MCP), prompt caching, deterministic validation (Zod/Pydantic), and human-in-the-loop (HITL) gates.
  • Direct communication: Dedicated Slack channel for rapid technical escalations and asynchronous guidance.

Best for: growing teams, startups, and mid-market organizations needing technical leadership and staff mentorship. Length: monthly retainer (scoped to your weekly hourly needs).
