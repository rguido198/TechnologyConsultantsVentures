# Technology Consultants — Design System & Brand Guidelines

**Repository:** Technology Consultants Ventures (`technologyconsultants.ventures`)  
**Brand Identity:** Modern B2B Software Engineering & Applied AI Practice — *"We build technology around your people — not instead of them."*

---

## 1. Brand Philosophy & Positioning

Technology Consultants is a premium technical practice founded by **Roberto Guido** (UC San Diego Electrical Engineering + NYU Stern MBA). We build custom React web applications, enterprise systems integrations (Workday Finance & Adaptive Planning), and applied AI automations with human-in-the-loop safety controls.

### Core Visual & Brand Principles
1. **Human Technology (People-First Automation):** Technology designed to clear routine work off people's plates so they can focus on high-judgment decisions. Human-in-the-loop (HITL) wherever trust, money, or judgment is on the line.
2. **Modern, Clean, High-Tech Aesthetics:** Warm, elegant off-white canvas (`#FBFAF5`), deep charcoal text (`#16161A`), crisp typography, rounded card containers, and smooth action pill buttons.
3. **Clean System Sans Typography:** `Helvetica Neue` / `Helvetica` / `Arial` sitewide for display titles, body text, buttons, and subheads. Uncluttered, highly readable, confident, and professional.
4. **Code-First Rigor:** Real working software proven on live self-developed products (*Appt Helper*, *Open Cita*, *Border Bills*).

## 3. Typography Standards

### Font Families
- **Display, Headlines, Body & UI:** `"Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, -apple-system, sans-serif` (Clean, modern system sans sitewide).
- **Monospace Accents:** `"IBM Plex Mono", ui-monospace, monospace` (Self-hosted; used strictly for technical code tags and status badges).

### Typography Rules
- **Display Headlines (H1):** Bold (`700`), Deep Charcoal (`#16161A`), `-0.02em` tracking.
- **Card Subheads (H2 / H3):** Bold (`700`), Deep Charcoal (`#16161A`).
- **Proof Bar Subheads:** Bold (`700`), Deep Charcoal (`#16161A`), e.g., **In production.**, **Senior team.**, **Code first.**, **One team.**
- **Body Copy:** Regular (`400`), `#454337`, line height `1.5`.

## 4. Component Guidelines

### 1. Navigation Header Bar
- **Background:** Sticky `--ink-dark` (`#121216`) with bottom border stroke `#26262C`.
- **Brand Mark:** Blue rounded square (`#2B59D9`) with cursor symbol (`›`).
- **Brand Name:** `Technology Consultants` in bold white sans.
- **Nav Links:** `Services` · `Work` · `How we work` · `About` · `FAQ` · `ES · Español`.
- **Action Pill:** White rounded pill button `Book a call`.

### 2. Buttons & Action Links
- **Primary Action Pill:** `#16161A` solid dark pill, white text, pill radius (`border-radius: 999px`), right arrow icon (`→`).
- **Secondary Ghost Pill:** `#FBFAF5` background, `#C9C3B2` line border, `#16161A` text.

### 3. Proof Principles Bar (4 Columns)
- **In production.** Real users today on our self-developed products.
- **Senior team.** The people who pitch your project ship it.
- **Code first.** No decks before working software.
- **One team.** Interfaces and automations: same engineers, same stack.

## 5. PowerPoint (.PPTX) Presentation Design Rules

Slide decks must be 100% representative of the website's brand identity:

1. **Font Family:** `Helvetica Neue` (or `Helvetica` / `Arial`) across ALL text elements (titles, subtitles, card headers, body text, buttons). No serif or typewriter fonts.
2. **Slide Background:** Warm Off-White Canvas `#FBFAF5`.
3. **Card Styling:** White rounded rectangles (`#FFFFFF`) with thin, soft borders (`#E2DDD0`).
4. **Header Navigation Mockup:** Top dark navigation bar (`#121216`) with blue brand icon (`›`) and white text.
5. **Action Buttons & Badges:** Black rounded pill buttons (`#16161A`) with white text and right arrows (`→`), and clean category badges.
6. **Footer:** Subtle line divider (`#E2DDD0`), `technologyconsultants.ventures · San Diego · Tijuana`, and page counter (`0X / 08`).
